About Company

We are an early stage startup developing a new authentication system using EEG signals. With these signals we are able to authenticate users passively without any direct input. So while a user is inputting their password, we can authenticate them simultaneously. Thus removing the need for any device verifications or one time passcodes or alternative biometric authentications that take time and are not as secure. 

These signals also provide us with another benefit. We are able to detect if users are under and mental duress. And then depending on the application the user can be restricted based on that. Our system is able to detect early stage signs of mental health disorders, symptoms related to drug or alcohol usage and many more. This can be very important for the trucking industry preventing possible accidents as a result of intoxication or other mental stresses. Can also help in military applications, making sure that the soldier getting the weapons is not under any mental duress that would impair their judgment.
